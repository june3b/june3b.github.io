ABOUT US

The anchorage autoshop - Founded in 2019, started trading in Oil products and Vehicle parts in 2020.We have a countrywide footprint with head offices in Area 6, Lilongwe, Branches in Biwi Lilongwe, Corner Mart building Limbe, chinyonga, Blantyre and Zomba.


We are the supllier of original European automotive parts and german oils in malawi and of highly specialized products from the worlds top manufacturers in automotive, mining, industrial and agricutural sectors.
We are also the official resellers of FUCHS Oils German, SHELL Oils and POWERTEX Batteries in Malawi.
