What is A Business Consultant?

A business consultant is an individual who works closely with business owners and managers who want to improve operations and efficiency, grow their businesses or maintain existing positive trends. Business consultants act as partners in identifying and overcoming the obstacles that arise along a company’s path to meet its goals.
