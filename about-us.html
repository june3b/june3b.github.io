Who We Are

Finance Management Experts

VCG is a financial and management firm that specializes in serving Micro, Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (MSMEs). At VCG, we are dedicated to providing personalized, high-quality services that help MSMEs navigate the complexities of finance and management and achieve their business goals.

At VCG, our team is committed to building long-lasting relationships with clients. We take the time to understand each client's unique needs, goals, and challenges, and we work collaboratively with our clients to develop tailored solutions that meet their specific requirements.

The VCG’s commitment to excellence, collaboration and community involvement makes us a valuable resource for businesses looking to achieve their financial and strategic goals and thrive in today’s competitive marketplace.

We are value-driven organization. Our values reflect the thinking of our founder and CEO,
Geoffrey V. Mtika who is the major force in shaping the firm. Our values inform both our long-term strategy as a firm and the way we serve our clients on daily basis.

1. Professionalism: We strive to maintain a high level of professionalism in all aspects of our
work, including our interactions with clients, our approach to problem-solving, and our adherence to ethical standards

2. Client-focused: We place a strong emphasis on understanding and meeting the unique needs of each client. We work closely with clients to develop tailored solutions that address their specific challenges and goals.

3. Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings. We are committed to upholding ethical standards and maintaining the trust of our clients.

4. Collaboration: We believe that teamwork and collaboration are essential to achieving success. We work closely with clients to understand their needs and goals, and we collaborate internally to bring together the right expertise and resources to address each client's unique challenges.

5. Innovation: We embrace new ideas and new technologies to enhance our services and provide clients with cutting-edge solutions. We innovate, test and learn from failure. We continuously seek out new ideas and approaches to add value and stay ahead of the curve.


6. Diversity and inclusion: We value diversity and inclusion and we strive to create a culture that is welcoming and supportive of all individuals. We recognize that diverse perspectives and backgrounds are essential to delivering the best possible outcomes for our clients.


At VCG, our mission is to provide personalized, high-quality consulting services that help Micro, Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (MSMEs) succeed in today's competitive marketplace. We are committed to building strong, long-term relationships with our clients, based on trust, integrity, and accountability, and to delivering innovative, tailored solutions that meet their unique needs


Our vision at VCG is to be the leading provider of tax, accounting, and strategic planning services for MSMEs, known for our exceptional quality, unparalleled expertise, and unwavering commitment to client success. We strive to be a strategic partner for our clients, providing the insights, guidance, and support they need to navigate complex business challenges and seize new opportunities.
